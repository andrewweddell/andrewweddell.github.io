Community Underwriting

Insuring Not For Profits - It's All We Do

NSW Meals on Wheels has been involved in helping Not for Profits obtain effective insurance for more than 20 years. What started from a few Meal Services, with difficulty obtaining insurance 25 years ago, has today grown to providing insurance to around 900 Not for Profits across Australia and more than $3 million in annual premiums.
We formed Community Underwriting in 2014 to take the CRISP Not for Profit insurance facility one step further – to become Australia’s only insurance provider for Not for Profits which is owned by its own clients. All CRISP Not for Profit insurance clients were insured by Calliden and managed by the Community Underwriting team in our initial arrangements, ensuring a seamless transition from the CRISP facility.

The Challenge

The challenge was to provide a flexible web base insurance solution which would be deployed in the Azure cloud. The platform needed to provide all features and functions for Community underwriting toQuote, Underwrite, Bill and manage all policies that were being sold to their customers via Broker and Direct channels. In addition, the system needed to provide straight through processing and digitise the entire life cycle of the sales and administration process.

Community Underwriting appointed Appsure to implement their solution and to partner with them in developing and implementing the Appsure solution.

The solution

Result

Appsure set about configuring and enhancing Appsure and its services to enable the selling of the Community Underwriting suite of products.

These products, sold through broker and direct channels include:

  • Association Liability Package

  • Business Insurance Package

  • General Liability

  • Motor Vehicle

  • Voluntary Workers Personal Accident

  • Industrial Special Risks

  • Event Cancellation Policy

  • Corporate Travel

  • Landcare NSW Scheme

The Appsure solution enables the full end to end process to be fully digitised with built in Underwriting, Rating, Automated letter generation, debit order, payment services and Claims.

The solution also provides a full report suite in Power BI which also generates written and paid bordereaux which are sent to the relevant insurance companies who hold the risk on a scheduled basis.The project included the migration of all historic business off Community Underwriting’s legacy system.

Client Testimonial
